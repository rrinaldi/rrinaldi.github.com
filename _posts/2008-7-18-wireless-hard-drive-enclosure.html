I’m a guy without a desktop.  At home I only use laptops, which is great and all but it comes with a couple downsides.  One of those downsides is that the collection of old hard drives that I have has been almost completely useless.  I know I could use a external enclosure but that is pretty pointless when I have my laptops so I can roam around my apartment unencumbered by cords and accessories.  For people like me, DLink has come out with an external hard drive enclosure that runs over a wireless network!  This is great!  Not only can it be used as an enclosure but it also allows you to add up to two USB 2.0/1.1 drives for more storage and can be used as a wireless access point.  This is going on my list of things to buy.
