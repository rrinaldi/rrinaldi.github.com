If you ever find yourself hankering for some sushi and you happen to be in Chicago's south loop, I would highly recommend Oysy Sushi at Michigan and 9th.  Richard turned me onto the place about 6 months ago or so and I've always been very impressed with the quality of the sushi and the excellent service.  The wonderful part is that I've been there quite a few times and I've never had to wait for a table, so I think it is still undiscovered.  I'm hesitant to even talk about the it because I don't want to wind up waiting for a table the next time I head over there.  :)
